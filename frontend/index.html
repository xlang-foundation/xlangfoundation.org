XLang

XLang, an open-source programming language, is crafted to empower AI applications for the Internet of Things (IoT), often characterized by its large-scale, distributed, and heterogeneous nature.

XLang Foundation

XLang is an open-source project created by XLang Foundation. We invite contributions from a global community of developers. Its GitHub repository (XLang Foundation GitHub) is a hub for collaboration, where developers can fork, watch, and star the project, engaging actively in its evolution. The XLang Foundation encourages developers to join their organization, participate in the project, and contribute to this pioneering effort in AI and IoT programming.
